First post

For the first post I wanted to talk about why I thought of making this and what I intend to do with it.

Why?

Honestly I was just hella bored, I have lots of things to do, but nothing feels fulfilling. I've also been trying to move away from what I consider "easy" dopamine, stuff like instagram reels and youtube shorts, and shift towards a more mindful way of consuming content.

Thus the blog, it gives me a creative outlet, lets me brush up my writing skills again, and forces me to be reflect on the content I consume when I write about it.

What?

So what am I going to put in here? I'd like to keep it flexible, so just talk about random stuff, maybe review some of the content I consume (games, books, movies, shows, anime etc.).

I guess it could also be a digital journal of sorts, maybe I post about stuff that's happened lately or something I came up with (a stupid poem, idea for a story, or just a project idea).

A few things to keep in mind going forward: - I'm not writing for anyone else, I am the primary reader, and as such I'm not going to focus on making it enteraining or anything. - I'm not going to force a schedule for posting. I feel that tends to lead to burnout, and I'd rather just post whenever I feel like it. - I need to avoid over-engineering the site, I already spent 2 days just going in circles over-engineering a basic blog site, and never actually writing anything.

Alright then, I'll see you (me) in the next post (whenever that is)!
